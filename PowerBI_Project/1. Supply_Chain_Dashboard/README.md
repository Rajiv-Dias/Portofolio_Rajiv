# 🚀 Supply Chain & Logistics Dashboard | Power BI

An end-to-end data analytics project focused on analyzing supply chain performance using an interactive Power BI dashboard. This project demonstrates the ability to transform raw data into actionable business insights for operational and strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

To analyze and optimize supply chain performance by identifying demand patterns, delivery inefficiencies, and logistics bottlenecks through data-driven insights.

---

## 📊 Business Problem

Companies often face challenges in:
- Fluctuating customer demand  
- Shipment delays across multiple carriers  
- Inefficient inventory management  

This dashboard addresses these issues by providing a centralized view of key supply chain metrics.

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- **Demand Volatility**  
  Order quantity fluctuates throughout the year, with a significant spike at the end of the period, indicating seasonal demand patterns.

- **Logistics Inefficiency**  
  Shipment delays are not limited to one carrier—Maersk, DHL, and DB Schenker show the highest delays, suggesting broader coordination issues.

- **Operational Gap**  
  Despite high delivery volume, delayed shipments remain significant, highlighting inefficiencies in logistics performance.
